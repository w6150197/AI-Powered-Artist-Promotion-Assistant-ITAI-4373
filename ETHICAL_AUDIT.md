Ethical Compliance Audit
Principles
1.	Human-in-the-Loop
o	No automated DMs or posts are sent.
o	Artist must approve all AI-generated suggestions.
2.	Data Privacy
o	Only public engagement data or artist-provided CSV is used.
o	Minimal personal information stored (username/handle + engagement metrics).
o	Compliant with GDPR and CCPA guidelines.
3.	Transparency
o	AI outputs are logged and auditable.
o	Artist is fully aware of how suggestions are generated.
4.	Intellectual Property
o	All artwork and creative content remain 100% property of the artist.
o	AI only generates text suggestions; artwork is never modified.
5.	Bias & Fairness
o	No recommendations based on sensitive characteristics (race, gender, religion, sexual orientation).
o	AI models trained/prompted to avoid unfair profiling.
6.	Platform Compliance
o	Only official APIs used.
o	No scraping beyond what is permitted.
o	All AI operations comply with Instagram, TikTok, and other platform terms of service.
7.	Audit Trail
o	Logs stored locally or in secure database.
o	Outputs can be reviewed and corrected by the artist.
8.	Ethical Oversight
o	Team review before deployment.
o	Clear instructions for artist on responsible use.
